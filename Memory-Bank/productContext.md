# Product Context: Shannon's Softball Recruiting Portfolio

## Why This Project Exists
College softball recruitment is highly competitive, with coaches receiving hundreds of player profiles. A well-designed portfolio website helps Shannon stand out by:
- Providing a comprehensive view of her abilities in one place
- Allowing coaches to easily access highlight videos and statistics
- Creating a professional first impression
- Enabling easy contact and follow-up

## Problems It Solves
1. **Information Scatter**: Instead of coaches piecing together information from various sources, everything is centralized
2. **Video Access**: Highlight videos are easily accessible and well-organized
3. **Professional Presentation**: Creates a polished, modern impression that reflects dedication
4. **Mobile Accessibility**: Coaches can review information on any device, anywhere
5. **Contact Barriers**: Removes friction for coaches to reach out

## How It Should Work
- **First Impression**: Hero section immediately shows Shannon's name, position, and key photo
- **Easy Navigation**: Clear menu allows coaches to jump to specific sections
- **Quick Access**: Key stats and contact info are prominently displayed
- **Video Showcase**: Highlight videos are categorized and easy to play
- **Professional Design**: Clean, modern aesthetic that reflects athletic professionalism

## User Experience Goals
- **Coaches can quickly assess Shannon's abilities** within 30 seconds
- **Videos load fast** and play smoothly on any device
- **Contact is effortless** with one-click email functionality
- **Information is scannable** with clear headings and visual hierarchy
- **Mobile experience is seamless** for coaches on tablets/phones

## Key User Journeys
1. **Quick Assessment**: Coach lands on site → sees hero section → checks stats → watches highlights → contacts
2. **Deep Dive**: Coach explores all sections → reviews academic info → watches multiple videos → downloads packet
3. **Reference**: Coach returns to site → finds specific video or stat → shares with other staff

## Success Indicators
- Low bounce rate (coaches stay on site)
- High video engagement (multiple videos watched)
- Contact form usage or email clicks
- Positive feedback from coaches
- Increased recruitment interest
